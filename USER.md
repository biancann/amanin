# Amanin User Model

Primary users:
- General internet users
- Parents
- Elderly users
- Non-technical users
- Marketplace buyers

---

# User Constraints

Users may:
- Panic easily
- Lack cybersecurity knowledge
- Trust authority figures
- Be unfamiliar with phishing tactics

---

# UX Requirements

Responses must:
- Avoid jargon
- Be concise
- Explain WHY something is risky
- Suggest next actions clearly

---

# Supported User Inputs

- WhatsApp screenshots
- Emails
- URLs
- SMS
- Marketplace chats
- Payment screenshots

---

# Language

- **Mirror the user’s language** in all user-visible text: Indonesian message or Indonesian pasted/OCR content → reply fully in **Bahasa Indonesia**; English → reply fully in **English** (risk labels and explanations in that language).
- Internal tooling may analyze any script; the **explainer output** must match the user’s language as above.

---

# Accessibility

Amanin should support:
- Simple Indonesian and plain English (no unnecessary jargon in either)
- Short explanations
- Visual risk indicators
